Color Scheme

Glaucous and Xiketic is the site's primary color and will be applied to the nav and the footer.

Prussian blue is a secondary color that will be used on important elements that are not the nav or the footer, such as buttons and headers.

Queen blue and baby powder are both accent colors which will be used on elements that should stand out and on things like borders and the page background.
