White Space and Clean Design

Apple

Apple Link

Apple's website uses white space to bring focus to the product they are showcasing, the design is clean and uncluttered and is very visually appealing. The white space creates a contrast for the viewer to focus on the point of the site, which is to be amazed on their product line ups.
