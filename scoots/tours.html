Tours

We are your one stop shop for touring Cozumel. Whether you would like help finding the beauty available on the island or want to take things at your own pace, we are here to help.
Cozumel awaits.

Seaside Tour

The waters surrounding Cozumel are a vision. We will take you to some of the most scenic spots for your ocean viewing pleasure and we challenge you to describe the beauty of the ocean waters.

Lush Forest Tour

Besides the incredible ocean views, Cozumel hosts a whole other terrain waiting for exploration. Enjoy the beauty of the forest, view the local wildlife and enjoy the mysteries of the island only the locals know.

Nature Tour

Join other nature lovers on this informational tour of the Cozumel Wildlife Preserve. Guides are knowledge about the local flora and fauna. Areas set up to allow you the opportunity to catch the perfect landscape picture.

Day Long Jeep Tour

This tour is all encompassing. Includes areas of interest from all the tours. Lunch is provided at a secret grotto with time to swim in the crystal clear waters found there. Fall in love with Cozumel.
