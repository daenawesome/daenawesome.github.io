Confirmation

Thank you for making your reservation with us. The only think left to do is pay your deposit. For your convenience we accept Google Pay, cash, credit card, debit card, or PayPal. Once your payment is secured, you will recieve an email from us confirming your reservation. Please be sure to check your spam folder as to not miss the email from us. It will have your confirmation number which you will need when picking up your rental. If you select cash, we will complete your reservation in person when you drop the deposit off.
