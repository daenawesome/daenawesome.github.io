Typography

The fonts I have chose to use are, Permanent Marker, and Nanum Gothic. Nanum Gothic is a contemporary sans-serif typeface with a warm touch, and it is expertly hinted for screen use. It is part of the Nanum fonts (나눔글꼴) – a set of high quality Unicode fonts designed especially for the Korean-language script “Hangeul” that also support Latin. Designed by Sandoll Communications (산돌 커뮤니케이션) and Fontrix (폰트릭스,) and published by Naver (네이버.) While Permanent Marker represents the look and feel of a favorite writing instrument.
