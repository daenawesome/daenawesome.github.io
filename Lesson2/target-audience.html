Target Audience

Audience Description

The target audience for my website are different people such as families and individuals ranging from the age of 18 and up. Their education is from High School and up. This group could include farmers who rely very much on the weather in order to make decisons about their day to day operations.

The site also targets those who love outdoor activities like hiking, ice skating, ski biking, jogging and so forth. Both residence and non residence of the state will benefit from this Website, it could be tourists planning to spend some time on a vacation or it could be a resident checking the weather in order to prepare for work.
